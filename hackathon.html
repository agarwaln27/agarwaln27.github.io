Overview

Providing patients with digital access to important discharge information and an avatar to care for using gamification and understandable instructions.

Challenge

How can we simplify the discharge process for young patients and their families so critical instructions are provided in an accessible manner?

Solution

With the simple placement of a QR code on the discharge papers, our solution provides patients and their families with digital access to their post-hospitalization instructions.

The instructions are formatted in a dashboard and include our innovation, "Charlie." Similar to a tamagotchi, Charlie is an avatar that the patient takes care of. This avatar mimics the patient's condition and treatment plan. In taking care of the avatar in a fun way, the patient will ultimately learn more about their own condition and how to take care of themselves.

Process

Within 24 hours, our team got to know one another, conducted research via several interviews with physicians, nurses, and hospital administrators, produced several ideas to address our chosen pain point, validated the QR code and avatar concept, and built high-fidelity wireframes to communicate the idea to stakeholders.
