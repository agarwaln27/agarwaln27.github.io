Fitness & Health Dashboard
General Assembly UX Design Course, Final Project

Timeline

4 months, April to July, 2015

Target Users

Health-conscious individuals

My Role
  • User research
  • Rapid prototyping & testing
  • Visual design

Overview

A mobile app that displays metrics gathered from the iPhone's motion and fitness sensor in a customizable dashboard.

Challenge

How can we help people achieve fitness goals using their basic fitness and health metrics?

Solution

Using myHealthDash, users can achieve their fitness goals without a wearable and simply by using the motion and fitness sensor in their phone. The data is gathered in a customizable dashboard, with a user-friendly layout and components for meaningful tracking and reflection.

Process

Over the four months of the General Assembly UX Design part-time course, I completed the various phases of the UX design process to create an app that would resonate with users. I created a site map to organize the components of the app, personas to validate needs and pain points, wireframes to iterate, and more.
