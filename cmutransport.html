Overview

Providing a better planning solution for students to compare all possible transportation options to and from campus without having to switch apps.

Challenge

How can we help students who live off-campus compare routes and plan their transportation better?

Solution

The CMU Go! app allows users to see end-to-end route options from origin to destination for the Pittsburgh buses in addition to the CMU bus and escort services, track buses in real-time, and get reminders for when to get off at their stop.

Process

Throughout the 4 months spent on this project, my team and I engaged in many research methods to uncover important needs in the CMU ridership community and ultimately generate a viable concept for addressing a subset of those needs. We conducted the following methods: stakeholder analysis, think alouds on existing products, contextual inquiries, affinity diagrams, speed dating, and stakeholder walkthrough.
